#  Gravity Bumpers

**Gravity Bumpers** is a cross-platform multiplayer arena brawler where your only goal is to survive. Control your sphere, use physics to your advantage, and bump your opponents off the platform into the void!

## Gameplay Features
*   **Physics-Driven Combat**: Master the momentum of your bumper to deliver devastating hits.
*   **Dash & Jump**: Use tactical abilities to recover or initiate attacks.
*   **Dynamic Arenas**: Battle on platforms that challenge your movement and precision.

## Connectivity
*   **Steam Integration**: Play with friends easily via Steam Lobbies and Invitations *(Desktop-only)*.
*   **LAN Support**: No internet? No problem. Full ENet-based local network support.
*   **Cross-Platform Ready**: Optimized for Desktop and Mobile (Android) with dedicated touch controls.

## Building with Steam support
To get **Gravity Bumpers** running in your editor and build it with Steam support, follow these steps:
### 1. Requirements
 * **Godot Engine 4.7** (Standard version recommended).
 *  **Steam Client** must be installed and running on your machine.

### 2. Installing GodotSteam (GDExtension)
This project requires the **GodotSteam** extension to handle Steam multiplayer lobbies and networking.
1. Download the [GodotSteam GDExtension](https://godotengine.org/asset-library/asset/2445) from the Asset Library.
2. In the Godot Editor, click on the **Asset Store** tab at the top.
3. Click the **Import** button in the top-right corner.
4. Select the `.zip` file you just downloaded.
5. Ensure the files are extracted into the `res://addons/godotsteam/` directory.
6. Restart the Godot Editor.
